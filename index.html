Who Am I?

Hi there! My name is Simran and I will be commencing my third year of studying Computer Science at the University of Waterloo this fall. I am also in the co-operative program and plan on specializing in business. This summer I am interning as an Associate Software Developer for Investors Group in downtown Toronto. I am also seeking a winter internship as a software developer or product management intern from January-April 2018. You can view my resume here.

Travel is a huge part of my life and I consider myself a global citizen, as I was born in India, raised in New Zealand, went to high school in Vancouver, and now I live in the Greater Toronto Area. I enjoy travelling and have been fortunate enough to visit destinations such as Australia, Chile, and numerous states in America. In my spare time I like to watch The Mindy Project, listen to hip hop music, kickbox, zumba, and try out new restaurants. I am also a huge Bollywood fan.

Playlist of the Month

Listening to music is an important aspect of my life and I enjoy compiling playlists inspired by seasons, moods, etc. Below is my playlist of the month, which includes 10 tracks that have been on repeat for me this month.

The most influential track for me this month is "1-800-273-8255" by Logic featuring Alessia Cara. The title of the song is actually the National Suicide Prevention Lifeline for America. Rapper Logic takes the listener through the mind of someone calling the helpline. I found the song to be catchy, emotional, and very well produced. Alessia's vocals were a nice touch.
